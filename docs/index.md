# TV Shared Lists

**Track, rate, and share your TV shows — all in one place.**

TV Shared Lists is a free iPhone and iPad app that lets you build a personal TV watchlist, rate the shows you've seen, and instantly share your list with friends via AirDrop, iMessage, or any other app.

---

## Features

- **Build your watchlist** — Search millions of shows and add them to your list with one tap
- **Rate what you've watched** — Star ratings (0–5) plus a Loved It / Not For Me thumbs indicator
- **Track your status** — Mark shows as Watched or Want to Watch
- **Share your list** — Export to CSV and share via AirDrop, iMessage, or email
- **Import from anywhere** — Import a CSV file or paste any direct link (including Google Drive and Google Sheets)
- **Works offline** — Your list is stored on your device; no account required
